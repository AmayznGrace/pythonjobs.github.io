Overview

Bank of America Merrill Lynch is looking to recruit a drive and passionate Software Developer to join our highly accomplished development team in London. You will be responsible for the development of technical solutions for the Complex FX Options (CFXO) desk and to work on our bespoke cross asset class system Quartz.

Quartz is Bank of America Merrill Lynch’s, primarily Python built, main platform for pricing trades, managing positions and computing risk exposure across all asset classes.

This is an excellent opportunity to work in a passionate and highly talented development team, where you can work on cutting edge, highly business critical systems. You will directly interact with Quantitative Analysts and the Front Office on a daily bases.

As a Software Developer your main responsibilities will involve:

  • Implementing technical solutions for Complex FX Options Technology (CFXO). May include developments for the global (London, Hong Kong and New York) risk export platform, product pricing, trade capture, feeds to downstream systems, implementing new strategic solutions
  • Gaining exposure to complex FX products through use of quantitative analytics libraries and interactions with quant/strat group
  • Developing mainly in Python, with possible exposure to C++, Shell Scripting, SQL. Platform is Linux/Unix and Quartz – Bank of America’s strategic, cross-asset, front–to-back position management, pricing, and risk management platform
  • Interacting with users globally across Trading, Middle Office, Quantitative Analytics

As a Software Developer your skills and qualifications will ideally include:

  • Ideally knowledge of Python, C++ or any structured programming language
  • Understanding of Object Oriented concepts and design patterns
  • Experience/understanding of querying databases/SQL
  • Exposure to both Linux/Unix and Windows development environments
  • Good Communication skills

About Bank of America Merrill Lynch

Bank of America is one of the world’s leading financial institutions, serving individual consumers, small- and middle-market businesses, large corporations and governments with a full range of financial and risk management products and services. Bank of America Merrill Lynch is the marketing name for the global banking and markets businesses.

The company has had a presence in EMEA since 1922. With offices in 23 countries on three continents, it offers an integrated and comprehensive set of products and services across Global Corporate and Investment Banking, Global Markets and Consumer Card, serving the needs of individual, corporate, institutional and government clients, combining the best of local knowledge and global expertise. Developing solutions for social and environmental challenges is at the core of Bank of America Merrill Lynch’s responsibility platform. In more than 90 countries around the world, we partner with employees, clients and stakeholders to help make financial lives better.
