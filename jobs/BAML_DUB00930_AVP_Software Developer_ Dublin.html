Overview

Bank of America Merrill Lynch’s Treasury Technology team is looking to recruit a driven and passionate AVP, Python Developer in Dublin. You will be responsible for designing and developing Python based solutions for a strategic in house Treasury application.

This is an excellent opportunity to develop a strategic in-house bank technology framework which utilizes Python, where you can develop your technical knowledge and build your career further.

As a Python Developer your main responsibilities will involve:

  • Developing Python solutions for our business clients.
  • Supporting existing Real time functionality and expanding upon it for new feature requests.
  • Providing lights on maintenance to ensure smooth running of existing application
  • Managing Software release into production.

As a Python Developer your skills and qualifications will ideally include:

  • 3+ Years of Python development experience
  • Experience with Object oriented design concepts and patterns
  • Experience with unit tests and TDD
  • Experience with NOSQL databases
  • Experience with in-house bank development platform such as Sec DB, Hydra or Quartz
  • Knowledge and experience of Agile practices (particularly Scrum).

Overview

Bank of America is one of the world’s largest financial institutions serving individual consumers, small and middle market businesses and large corporations with a full range of banking, investing, asset management and other financial and risk-management products and services.

The company serves clients in more than 150 countries and has relationships with 99 percent of the US Fortune 500 companies and 83 percent of the Fortune Global 500. Bank of America Corporation stock (NYSE: BAC) is a component of the Dow Jones Industrial Average and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.

Bank of America has maintained a presence in the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region since 1922. Bank of America employs over 14,000 people in Europe with approximately 630 employed in Ireland.
