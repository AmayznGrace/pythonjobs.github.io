Overview

We are looking for an experienced Python Developer to help us build and scale our Financial Management application within the bank’s Quartz platform. Our team is responsible for delivering Product Control solutions which allows the business to monitor, investigate and report on large volumes of trading activities within the bank.

The successful candidate will play a major role in the full development cycle of our products, working within an Agile development process. He or she will also be expected to provide technical leadership in solution design, software development and testing.

Prior experience in the financial sector is useful but not required.

Requirements

  • Fluency in the Python language and standard libraries
  • Experience in designing and building scalable data-oriented applications
  • Experience in Test-driven Development and Continuous Integration
  • Experience with both Relational and Document Oriented databases

Great to haves

  • Experience working in a Agile environment
  • Experience in other languages e.g. Java, C++, C#, Scala
  • Experience with Grid Computing and High Performance Computing
