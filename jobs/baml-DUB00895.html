Overview

Bank of America Merrill’s is looking for an experienced Software Developer to join our Controls Technology Derivatives team in Dublin. This is an excellent opportunity to join a well-established, innovative team and to work on state-of-the-art, real-time, high availability systems in one of the most dynamic areas within one of the world’s largest banks. You will be able to contribute to the continuous development and evolution of business critical technologies; where you can work with up and coming, cutting edge technologies, such as Python.

As VP, Software Developer your main responsibilities will involve:

  • Developing large, business critical solutions
  • Supporting existing real time functionality and expanding upon it for new feature requests
  • Being innovative and creating bespoke solutions utilising a wide variety of technologies
  • Providing Technical leadership within the team
  • Writing automated tests
  • Mentoring and teaching others
  • Software release into production.

As VP, Software Developer your skills and qualifications will ideally include: • Extensive experience working with a variety of technologies to an advanced level, including scripting languages (especially Python) and databases (especially. MS SQL).

  • Proven ability to deliver Complex SSIS packages
  • Experience of training others technically and defining/driving/leading good practices.
  • Our preferred language is Python, but we have a variety of other technologies within our portfolio such as: SQL, SSIS, .Net, Java, etc.
  • Demonstrating Technical Leadership with experience in influencing and delivery technical change.
  • Proven track record of leadership within teams, providing Mentoring, Guidance and support to team members
  • Proficiency in writing automated tests.
  • Demonstrable experience in working with stakeholders and influencing business strategy through the use of technology
  • Knowledge and experience of Agile practices (particularly Scrum) would be very useful
  • Domain experience is not essential but would be beneficial: reconciliation and controls; operations technology within a large financial organisation; Futures and Options and OTC reporting

About Bank of America

Bank of America is one of the world’s largest financial institutions serving individual consumers, small and middle market businesses and large corporations with a full range of banking, investing, asset management and other financial and risk-management products and services. The company serves clients in more than 150 countries and has relationships with 99 percent of the US Fortune 500 companies and 83 percent of the Fortune Global 500. Bank of America Corporation stock (NYSE: BAC) is a component of the Dow Jones Industrial Average and is listed on the New York Stock Exchange. Bank of America has maintained a presence in the Europe, Middle East and Africa (EMEA) region since 1922. Bank of America employs over 14,000 people in Europe with approximately 630 employed in Ireland.
